Sort
===========
Time complexity of sorting: 
|            |   Best   |   Medium       |  Badly     |
|------------|:--------:|:--------------:|:-----------|
|Bubble:     | O(n)     |   O(n^2)       |  O(n^2)    |
|Selection:  | O(n^2)   |   O(n^2)       |  O(n^2)    |
|Insertion:  | O(n)     |   O(n^2)       |  O(n^2)    |
|QuickS:     | O(nlogn) |   O(nlogn)     |  O(n^2)    |
|Merge:      | O(nlogn) |   O(nlogn)     |  O(nlogn)  |
|Shell:      | O(n)     |   O(n*(logn)^2)|  O(n^(3\2))|
